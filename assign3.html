Frequently Asked Questions

What is Netflix?

Netflix is a popular online streaming service that offers a wide variety of TV shows, movies,documentaries, and other forms of content to its subscribers. It was founded in 1997 as a DVD-by-mail service but has since evolved into a global streaming platform.

With a monthly subscription fee, users can access Netflix's vast library of content and stream it on various devices, including smart TVs, gaming consoles, tablets, and smartphones. Netflix offers a personalized experience for each user, suggesting content based on their viewing history and ratings. In addition, Netflix has also produced many original TV shows and movies, which have gained critical acclaim and a large following.

How much does Netflix cost?

The cost of Netflix varies depending on the subscription plan you choose and the country you are in. Generally speaking, Netflix offers three main subscription plans: 1. Basic plan, 2.Standard plan and 3.Premium plan.

Where can I watch?

Netflix can be accessed from a variety of devices and platforms like smart TVs, streaming devices, computers, mobile devices and set-up boxes.

How do I cancel?

If you want to cancel your Netflix subscription, you can do so at any time. Here's how to cancel Netflix: Log in to your Netflix account using a web browser on your computer or mobile device. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner of the screen. Click on the "Account" option from the dropdown menu. Under the "Membership and Billing" section, click on the "Cancel Membership" option. Follow the prompts to complete the cancellation process. It's important to note that once you cancel your Netflix subscription, you'll still have access to the service until the end of your current billing period. After that, you won't be charged any further and your account will be deactivated. If you change your mind and want to resume your subscription at a later date, you can simply log back into your account and restart your subscription.
